Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about DoIT Technical Club

How can I become a member of the club?

Simply fill out our online membership form available on our website and attend our next event to get started.

Is there a cost associated with joining?

DoIT Technical Club Membership is free for all DoIT students.

Can members initiate their tech projects?

Absolutely! We encourage members to pitch project ideas, and the club provides resources and mentorship to help bring these projects to fruition. Just drop a message in the WhatsApp group to initiate the process.
