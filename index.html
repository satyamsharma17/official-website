Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) about DoIT Technical Club

How can I become a member of the club?

Simply fill out our online membership form available on our website and attend our next event to get started.

Is there a cost associated with joining?

DoIT Technical Club Membership is free for all DoIT students.

Can members initiate their tech projects?

Absolutely! We encourage members to pitch project ideas, and the club provides resources and mentorship to help bring these projects to fruition. Just drop a message in the WhatsApp group to initiate the process.

Approach

Our Approach.

Ideation & Discussion

01

Every initiative, be it a project or event, starts with an open brainstorming session. We encourage members to pitch ideas, discuss feasibility, and determine the impact. This ensures a bottom-up approach!

Planning & Allocation

02

Once an idea is approved, meticulous planning begins. We draft timelines, allocate resources, and designate roles. For projects, this could mean creating a development roadmap; for events, it involves logistics and scheduling.

Execution & Monitoring

03

With a clear plan in hand, we move to execution. Regular check-ins, progress tracking, and feedback loops are established to ensure that projects and events stay on course and any challenges are addressed promptly.

Review & Learn

04

Post-completion, we conduct a thorough review. This involves analyzing what went well, what can be improved, and how we can refine our approach for future endeavors. Every project and event is a learning opportunity, and we capitalize on it.
